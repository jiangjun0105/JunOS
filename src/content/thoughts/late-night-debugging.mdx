# Late-Night Debugging

There is a particular clarity that arrives past midnight, when the rest of the
world has gone quiet and the only sound is the fan spinning.

## Why it works

During the day, your attention is fractured — messages, meetings, the pull of
everything else. At night, the interruptions stop. The problem gets your full
bandwidth.

## The danger

The clarity is real, but the judgment is impaired. I've learned to write down
the insight, then verify it in the morning. Half the time it still holds. The
other half, it was caffeine talking.

## The ritual

1. Notice the bug.
2. Stare at it until midnight.
3. Suddenly see the one wrong assumption.
4. Fix it in three lines.
5. Doubt yourself until the tests pass.
