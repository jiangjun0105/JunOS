# Reading Group: Scaling Laws

Notes from a session on how loss scales with parameters, data, and compute.

## The one-line takeaway

Loss falls as a power law in each of model size, dataset size, and compute —
until one of them becomes the bottleneck.

<Embed src="https://youtu.be/aqz-KE-bpKQ" title="Placeholder talk (Creative Commons clip)" />

## Questions we argued about

- Which axis is _actually_ binding for the models we train?
- How far do the curves hold before they bend?
- What do they predict that we could falsify cheaply?

> A power law is a great extrapolation tool — right up until the moment it isn't.
