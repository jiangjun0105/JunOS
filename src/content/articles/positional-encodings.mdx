# Positional Encodings, Three Ways

Self-attention is permutation-invariant, so _something_ has to tell the model
where each token sits. Three families I keep comparing:

## Sinusoidal

Fixed sine/cosine waves at geometric frequencies — no parameters, and in
principle it extrapolates to sequences longer than anything seen in training.

## Learned

A trainable vector per position. Simple and strong in-distribution, but it can't
say anything about positions it never saw.

## Rotary (RoPE)

Rotates query/key vectors by an angle proportional to position, so the dot
product depends on _relative_ distance. My current default.
