# On Generalization

A grab-bag of intuitions I trust more than I can prove.

## Intuitions

1. Capacity without the right inductive bias just memorizes.
2. Data quality moves the needle more than another architecture tweak.
3. "It generalizes" usually means "it generalizes _to the test set you measured_".
